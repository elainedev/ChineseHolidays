About the Author

The author is an American of Chinese descent who spent her childhood in Taiwan, a small island off the coast of China. The author moved to the United States before middle school. The stories on this webpage were heard by the author while living in Taiwan from Chinese children's books, school, or oral narratives. Many of the traditions described were also experienced by the author growing up. The author misses many of the Chinese foods, but thankfully she currently lives in the San Francisco Bay Area where there is easy access to Asian markets and restaurants.
